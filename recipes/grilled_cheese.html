Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Grilled cheese sandwiches are a simple but efficient way to heal the proverbial soul when a person is feeling blue you should fill them with melted cheesy goo.

List of Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Mayo or Butter

Steps to Make a Grilled Cheese Sandwich:

  1. Heat up a skillet on medium heat.
  2. Spread butter or mayo onto one side of your bread.
  3. Let the bread toast on the un-buttered(mayoed) side.
  4. Flip the un-buttered(mayoed) side to the skillet
  5. Add two slices of cheese to one slice of bread then add the other slice of bread to the under construction sandwich, butter(mayo) side up.
  6. Cook on both sides with a lid on the skillet until they are golden brown.
  7. Remove from skillet.
  8. Let the sandwich cool.
  9. Eat sandwich.
