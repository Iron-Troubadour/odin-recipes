Brownies

List of Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 0.75 cup all-purpose flour
  • 0.66 cup cocoa powder
  • 0.5 cup powdered sugar
  • 0.5 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 0.75 teaspoons sea salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 0.5 cup canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 0.5 teaspoons vanilla
    •

    Steps to Make Brownies:

    1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees fahrenheit.
    2. Mix the dry and we ingredients in two separate bowls.
    3. Next combine the wet and dry ingredients together.
    4. Then pour the batter into an 8x8 inch baking pan lined with parchment paper.
    5. Put in oven and bake for 40 to 45 minutes, until a toothpick can be inserted and removed cleanly.
    6. Let cool and enjoy.
